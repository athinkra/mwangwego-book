FONTLOG

Athinkra Mwangwego Book font family
===================================
The "Mwangwego Book" typeface is derived directly from the "JG Oromo"
TrueType font created by Jason Glavy. The font was released under the OpenFont
Licese v1.1 on May 10, 2024.

All modifications to the original JG Oromo font are detailed in the following:


Version 1.700 - February 7m 2026
-------------
* Glyphs reencoded under the Unicode 19 provisional range for Mwangwego: U+16E00 - U+16E3F


Version 1.601 - September 21, 2024
-------------
* Combining glyphs will now "stack" instead of overlap when the occur in an inappropriate sequence.


Version 1.600 - September 15, 2024
-------------
* The initial OFL public release of the font with characters encoded in the 
   Supplementary Private Use Area-A.
